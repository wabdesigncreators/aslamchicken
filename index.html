Savor the Flavors of Aslam Chicken: Culinary Creations That Delight

Discover a world of flavor with Aslam Chicken's exquisite range of dishes, each crafted to tantalize your taste buds and elevate your dining experience. Our menu features a diverse selection of chicken creations, from succulent grilled masterpieces to hearty, spiced curries. Each dish is meticulously prepared with high-quality ingredients and a blend of unique spices, ensuring that every bite is a memorable delight. Whether you're in the mood for a comforting classic or an adventurous culinary journey, Aslam Chicken offers something for every palate. Dive into our collection and savor the perfect harmony of taste, texture, and tradition.

Fish Tikka

Fish tikka is a flavorful, spiced grilled dish with tender, juicy pieces of fish. It's typically marinated in a blend of yogurt and aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric, then cooked until slightly charred. The result is a deliciously smoky, spicy, and tangy treat that's both satisfying and refreshing.

kashmiri Kabab

Kashmiri kababs are rich and aromatic, featuring tender, minced meat infused with spices like saffron, cardamom, and cloves. They have a unique blend of savory and slightly sweet flavors, with a melt-in-your-mouth texture that's enhanced by a subtle smokiness from grilling.

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a rich, creamy Indian dish featuring tender chicken pieces simmered in a luscious tomato-based sauce with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices. It’s both flavorful and indulgent.
